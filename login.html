Sign In Options

The login options will be expanded in the future to include Facebook, Twitter, and eduGAIN.

For now, you need to register with a Google account.

  • Click on the Google button.
  • Choose an existing account if you have already set one up or "Use another account" if it is your first time logging in.
  • If you choose "Use another account" you will be asked to enter your Google email address or phone number. If you do not have a Google account, it will ask you if you want to create one. Click on "Create account" to create a new Google account.
